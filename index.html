Bash Woman Community

Bash Woman Community is an initiative to guide and mentor students. Many students are not aware of the tech and need some mentorship. We as a community do our part by mentoring students in different domains. The aim of our community is to help 10k people in 2021. It has already helped many people by organizing events, meetups, contests, and also through direct communication.

Events

Code A Byte

Code-A-Byte is the first competitive coding event that will be organized by Bash Woman Community. It will be hosted on Code Forces. Taking part in this contest will teach you how to be more focused on the task and not only complete it quickly, but accurately.

Blogging Ninja

Blogging Ninja is the next level technical blog writing challenge organized by Bash Woman Community. Technical blogs are the way to diversify our knowledge base and help the community. With the upcoming new technologies the older proposed solution needs to be reiterated, thus technical blogs come in aid.
